Aiki Budo of Denver offers Aikido and Kenjutsu training in Denver, Colorado.

Aiki Budo of Denver is affiliated with Aikido Shimbokukai, an Officially Recognized Organization of the Aikikai Foundation (Aikido World Headquarters) in Tokyo, Japan.
