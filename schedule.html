Fees

Monthly Dues

Monthly dues give you access to all our classes. The monthly dues are currently $120. These are payable during the first week of the month. We offer discounted pricing for full-time students and military veterans.

Aikido Shimbokukai Membership Dues

Aikido Shimbokukain membership dues are currently $65 per year, payable at the beginning of the year.

Visiting Aikidoka

The mat fee for visiting aikidoka is $20 per day.
