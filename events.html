Aikido Budo of Denver 6-Week Introduction to Aikido Beginners Program, January 15, 2019 to February 21, 2019

Aiki Budo of Denver's 6-week Introduction to Aikido Beginners Program starts January 15. It introduces new students to the principles, philosophy, basic techniques, and practice of Aikido. Classes will be held Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6 PM to 7:45 PM.

The total cost for the 6-week program is $99 including the uniform (dogi).

For more information, contact (720) 232-7605 or aikibudoofdenver@gmail.com.
