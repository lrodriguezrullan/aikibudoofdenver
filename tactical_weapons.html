Tactical Weapons Training

Tactical weapons training is practiced with knife, stick, staff, karambit, and other weapons. It is close quarters and offensive/defensive technical training with an integrated, yet simplistic approach. It includes movement and the wielding of multiple weapons as well as empty hand.

As is all training here at Aiki Budo of Denver, classes are all principle based, not solely on technical knowledge. Movement replaces predetermined technique as nothing can be preplanned. Preparation and strategy are critical, but once the conflict has begun, tactics must be of the body, not the mind.

Classes are conducted in street clothes, because that is what you will be wearing everyday on the street.
